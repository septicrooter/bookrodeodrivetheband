What Makes Us Different

Songs You Know. A Show You Won't Forget.

🎤

Sing It With Us

We play the songs everyone actually knows — the ones that make a crowd stop talking and start singing. Hotel California. Sweet Caroline. Jolene. Dreams. We pick songs that bring people together, not just fill space.

🕺

Built to Dance

The dance floor doesn't fill itself. We read the room, build the energy, and know exactly when to drop the song that gets everyone out of their chairs. By the end of the night, people who came to "just watch" are dancing.

🎷

Completely Unique

Live saxophone. Mandolin. Keys, electric guitar, and acoustic — all from the same guy. Four distinct voices, four real characters. There's no other band in Atlanta that sounds like us, because there's no other band built like us.

We're not a corporate package. We're not a tribute act. We're four real musicians who love what we do — and it shows. Every single night.
